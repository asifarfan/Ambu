Top Ambulance Services for Corporate









Dedicated Emergency Helpline


AmbuCare drivers are well-trained in first aid assistance and patient handling.

In-House Medical Rooms


Under this service, we set up a medical room with all the necessary equipment, and first aid tools along with highly trained Manpower, on-site.

Stationed Dedicated Ambulance


Under this service, AmbuCare provides GPS Enabled ambulances that are stationed 24*7, at the client's office premises and ready to serve the client during any emergency, these ambulances can take the patient to the nearest hospital within 10 mins in tier 1 cities. The ambulance is manned with trained drivers and paramedics and has all the necessary equipments, providing industry-standard healthcare to our clients.
